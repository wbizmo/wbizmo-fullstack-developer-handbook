# Fun Facts

1. Kubernetes was originally inspired by Google's internal Borg system.
2. Terraform configurations can manage infrastructure across multiple cloud providers.
3. Most major technology companies operate internal developer platforms.
4. SRE was pioneered at Google.
5. Platform engineering emerged partly as a response to DevOps scaling challenges.
6. Modern cloud regions contain multiple independent availability zones.
7. Large platforms often automate thousands of deployments per day.
8. Developer experience is now considered a platform engineering concern.
