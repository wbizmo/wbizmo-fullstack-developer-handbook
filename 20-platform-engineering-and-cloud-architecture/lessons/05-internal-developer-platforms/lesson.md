# Lesson 05: Internal Developer Platforms

## Learning Objectives

- Understand Internal Developer Platforms.
- Understand self-service infrastructure.
- Understand developer portals.
- Understand platform products.

---

## What Is An Internal Developer Platform?

An Internal Developer Platform (IDP) is a platform built for developers inside an organization.

Goal:

Provide self-service capabilities.

---

## Examples

Developers can:

- deploy applications
- provision databases
- view metrics
- access logs

without opening tickets.

---

## Benefits

- faster delivery
- reduced bottlenecks
- consistency
- improved developer experience

---

## Platform As Product

Modern platform teams treat platforms as products.

Users:

Developers.

Customer experience matters.

---

## Self-Service

Developer requests:

Database

↓

Platform provisions automatically.

No manual intervention.

---

## Components

Typical IDP includes:

- deployment system
- observability
- documentation
- service catalog
- infrastructure templates

---

## Summary

Internal Developer Platforms help organizations scale software delivery through self-service infrastructure and automation.
