# Lesson 01: Introduction To Platform Engineering

## Learning Objectives

- Define platform engineering.
- Understand platform teams.
- Understand developer experience.
- Understand self-service infrastructure.
- Understand platform responsibilities.

---

## What Is Platform Engineering?

Platform Engineering is the discipline of building tools, infrastructure and automation that enable developers to deliver software efficiently.

Platform engineers build platforms.

Developers build products on top of those platforms.

---

## Platform Engineering vs DevOps

DevOps:

Culture and practices.

Platform Engineering:

Product-oriented implementation of those practices.

---

## Internal Platforms

Examples:

- deployment platforms

## Goal

Reduce cognitive load for developers.

Developers should focus on:

Building products.

Not managing infrastructure.
