# Fun Facts

1. Distributed systems are often more limited by coordination than computation.
2. Consensus is one of the hardest problems in computer science.
3. Raft was designed to be easier to understand than Paxos.
4. Kubernetes relies heavily on etcd, which uses Raft.
5. Gossip protocols are inspired by how rumors spread through social groups.
6. Perfect clock synchronization is impossible.
7. Many cloud outages begin with coordination failures.
8. Most engineers use distributed systems daily without realizing it.
